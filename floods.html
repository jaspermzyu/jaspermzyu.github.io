Floods are when water extends over what is usually dry land. This can happen when it escapes from a natural watercourse, such as a lake, river or creek. FLoods can also happen when water is released for a reservior, canal or dam. There are two types of flooding: riverine and flash. One of the deadliest floods of all time was the the 1931 Yangste-Huai Rive Floods were the deadliest flood of all time killing as many as 4 million people in China.
