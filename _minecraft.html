Daring Dungeons Update

In this update Mojang added a brand new item that the mincraft community has gone wild about: The Mace. This is by far the most overpowered weapon mojang has added thus far as it has a base of 7 HP or 3 and a half hearts however every fallen block from two blocks up will do an additional 1.5 hearts of damage and you can literally one shot the warden if u fall from high enough. The Mace also cancels out all your fall damage when you are falling and hit a mob and when you are falling from more than five blocks then it issues a large knockback radius. The Mace can have these enchantments: Mending, Unbreaking, Smite, Bane of Arthopods, Fire Aspect as well as 3 new enchantments unique to the Mace. Density: This enchantment has five levels and with each level adding more damage when falling towards a target each block fallen which is usually 1.5 hearts now does 2 hearts as it adds 0.5 hearts every level.

Therfore if you have Density 5 you could total up to 5 hearts of damage per block fallen. It can be found by enchanting using an enchanting table or spawn in loot chests in structures with enchanted books in their loot table or from the ominous vault. Breach: This enchantment has 4 levels and each level reduces 15% of an armors effetiveness of the target. It can be found by enchanting using an enchanting table or spawn in loot chests in structures with enchanted books in their loot table. Windburst: This enchantment has 3 levels and each level created particles and sends the opponent higher up into the sky. This enchantment can literally take your opponent with you on a flight to the stars. It is only found by getting it from ominous vaults. Other than the mace minecraft also added a new structure called trial chambers which you can choose to be harder by drinking an ominous potion which when drinken all spawners become ominous spawners and you will get the ominous event effect which increases the difficulty and causes the affected spawners to have a 30% chance to drop ominous keys which can be used to open ominous vaults. The Mace is crafted by a breeze rod which is dropped by a new mob called the breeze and a heavy core which can be found in ominous vaults but only have 1.2% of being in the new ominous vaults.

The Trails and Tales Update

In this update Mojang added a item called a brush which is used to brush blocks and if you see gravel or sand with a suspicious texture, then use the brush on it and u can get a range of different thing such as pottery sherds. Armor Trims, there are 16 unique armor trims that you can use with different ingots to get different colors and each armor trim has a unique pattern that you can add to your armor. Not elytras though. You can duplicate trims by using seven diamonds and a unique block depending which armor trim you have. The Sniffer is a mob that you can hatch from eggs that u can find in the ocean structure other than the ocean monument and the shipwreck or the abandoned Nether Portal. The egg hatches quicker on moss. The sniffer can sniff out ancient seed such as the pitchflower seed or the torchflower seed. The Camel is a new mob that two people can ride on if the camel has a saddle as well as being able to dash up to 12 blocks. Hostile mobs cannot damage u with the exception of the warden, wither, enderdragon, guardians and elder guardians.
