  7. Thea, titaness of sigh, prophecy and all things that shimmer, Wife of Hyperion

  9. Mnemosyne, titaness of memory and rememberance and the inventress of language and words
  10. Phoebe, titaness of intelluct and prophecy

  1. Clotho weaved the Threads of Fate, the beginning of all life.
  2. Lahkesis would measeure the threads, determining how long a mortal, God, giants and Titans life span would be.
  3. Atropos cuts the threads, ending the life
